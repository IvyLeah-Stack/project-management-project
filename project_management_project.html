📋 Project Overview

Project Title: Community Health Awareness Campaign in Kibera

Duration: 6 months (January 2024 - June 2024)

Budget: KSH 500,000

Objective: To improve health awareness and access to basic healthcare information in Kibera informal settlement through community workshops, health screening, and educational materials distribution.
